Breakdown Visuels IA

Comment j'ai généré ces 4 visuels pub pour noobiotik

Le process complet — de l'inspiration jusqu'au prompt final — pour créer des visuels publicitaires IA qui ressemblent à de vraies photos de marque.

Avant de prompter la moindre image, il y a toute une phase de préparation. C'est ce qui fait la différence entre un visuel IA « qui se voit » et un visuel IA qui passe pour une vraie photo de marque. Voici la méthode exacte que j'ai suivie pour noobiotik.

01

Bibliothèque publicitaire Meta

Je commence toujours par la Meta Ad Library pour voir ce que la marque a déjà produit : codes visuels, ton, format, promesses mises en avant. Ça me donne le langage de la marque.

02

Recherche d'inspirations sur Pinterest

J'élargis le spotting visuel avec Pinterest : compositions, lumières, pastilles, typos. L'objectif n'est pas de copier, mais de nourrir une direction artistique cohérente.

03

Création d'un moodboard

Je rassemble toutes ces références dans un moodboard. C'est la base de travail — ce qui me permet de savoir exactement ce que je veux obtenir avant d'ouvrir quoi que ce soit d'autre.

04

Rédaction du prompt avec ChatGPT

Je demande à ChatGPT de me rédiger un prompt au format « nano banana » à partir d'une image de référence et de ce que je veux modifier. Le mot-clé ici : précision. Chaque détail doit être écrit — composition, cadrage, lumière, matière, texte à intégrer, camera, color grading, negative prompts. Si tu sautes un élément, l'IA comble les trous et tu perds le contrôle.

05

Génération sur Weavy

Je génère l'image dans Weavy en lui donnant le prompt + l'image de référence. C'est ce combo (référence visuelle + prompt ultra-détaillé) qui permet d'obtenir un résultat proche d'une vraie photo produit.

Les 4 visuels & leurs prompts

Clique sur « voir le prompt » pour déplier et copier celui que tu veux tester.

01

Produit sur socle — DÉTOX Foie

Le prompt
▾ 
A clean, minimalistic product advertising composition featuring a white supplement bottle with a matte silver cap and a vibrant green label (detox supplement style), placed on a soft beige ribbed pedestal tray. Two off-white capsules are lying naturally next to the bottle.

Front-facing composition, slightly angled product (10° rotation), centered but slightly lower in frame to allow text space above. Shot with an 85mm lens, eye-level perspective, medium close-up framing.

Background is a soft neutral marble surface with subtle grey veins, combined with vertical beige ribbed wall texture on the left side. Overall palette: warm beige, off-white, soft neutrals, contrasted by the vivid green label.

Lighting is soft studio diffused light, high-key setup, with gentle shadows under the bottle. Subtle bounce light from below to soften contrast. Clean commercial skincare lighting, no harsh reflections.

Text layout included in composition:
– Top center: elegant brand logo style (minimal, black)
– Handwritten style quote in black: "Ces (bonnes) bactéries m'ont aidé à retrouver mon confort intime"
– Left side badges: 4.6/5 rating with stars, and "Made in France" icon

Material realism:
matte plastic bottle with soft micro-scratches, slightly worn edges on label, subtle paper texture on label, capsules with semi-matte finish and slight translucency, faint dust particles on surface

No human presence.

Mood: premium wellness, clean, reassuring, feminine health aesthetic, French pharmacy branding style

Color grading: soft warm tones, slightly desaturated environment, green label remains vibrant and crisp

Camera:
shot with Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.2 lens, shallow depth of field, creamy background blur, high-resolution 8K capture

--no distorted label, warped text, extra logos, cluttered background, harsh shadows, overexposure
02

Témoignage UGC — « Sandra »

Le prompt
▾ 
A clean testimonial-style advertising image featuring a smiling blonde woman in her late 20s to early 30s, holding a white supplement bottle with a matte silver cap and a vibrant green label (detox supplement) between her fingers.

Composition:
medium close-up portrait, subject positioned on the right side of the frame, leaving negative space on the left for text. The woman is facing camera, natural posture, relaxed shoulders, genuine smile with visible teeth, lively and authentic expression.

The product is held near her face at chest level, clearly visible, label facing forward.

Text layout on the left side:
– Top: 5 black stars rating (★★★★★)
– Title in elegant serif: "Détox"
– Testimonial text:
"Rien à redire, je me sens bien, associé à d'autres cures, c'est top!"
– Name below: "Sandra"

Below the testimonial block:
– "4.6/5" rating in clean minimal style with subtle stars
– "Made in France" badge with minimalist map icon

Background:
clean off-white / light beige studio background, minimal, no texture, high-end skincare aesthetic

Lighting:
soft diffused studio lighting, high-key, even exposure, soft shadows, subtle glow on skin, beauty lighting setup

Subject styling:
blonde woman, natural makeup, light skin tone, soft pink lips, minimal imperfections, slightly wavy or straight natural hair, wearing a soft neutral or muted pink top

Material realism:
matte plastic bottle with subtle micro-abrasions, slightly textured green label, crisp typography, realistic reflections on cap

Human detail:
visible pores, fine skin texture, slight natural asymmetry in smile, subtle under-eye softness, natural skin translucency, faint peach fuzz

Mood:
authentic, reassuring, UGC-inspired but polished, wellness and trust-driven, French pharmacy brand tone

Color grading:
neutral and warm, soft desaturation overall, green label remains the main accent color

Camera:
shot with Nikon Z8, 50mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, sharp focus on face and product, soft background blur

--no distorted face, unrealistic hands, warped product, extra text, cluttered background, harsh lighting
03

Wellness torse — « Je me sens mieux »

Le prompt
▾ 
A clean, minimal wellness advertising image featuring a close-up of a feminine torso wearing a soft sage green seamless sports set (top and leggings), holding a white supplement bottle with a matte silver cap and a vibrant green label (detox supplement).

Composition:
tight mid-section framing (from chest to hips), centered composition. The bottle is held gently with both hands at stomach level, one hand resting on top of the cap, the other supporting from below. Natural, relaxed hand positioning.

The product label is clearly visible and facing forward.

Text layout:
– Top center, elegant serif typography:
"Je me sens mieux depuis que je fais cette cure"
(sentence case, refined, slightly italic, premium editorial style)

– Bottom center below the product:
"4.6/5 ★★★★★"
clean minimal typography, subtle and balanced

Background:
soft neutral off-white / light beige studio background, no distractions, high-end wellness aesthetic

Lighting:
soft diffused studio lighting, high-key, very smooth shadows, subtle highlight on skin contours and product edges. Beauty lighting with gentle gradient falloff.

Material realism:
matte plastic bottle with subtle micro-abrasions, slightly textured green label, realistic print sharpness, soft reflections on metallic cap

Human detail:
torso skin with visible pores, subtle skin texture, natural tone variation, slight warmth, soft abdominal definition, faint skin translucency, realistic hand texture with slight veins and natural imperfections

Mood:
clean, intimate wellness, body-focused, calm, reassuring, premium detox / gut health positioning

Color grading:
warm neutral tones, slightly desaturated overall palette, sage green outfit harmonized with vibrant green label, natural skin tones preserved

Camera:
shot with Sony A7R V, 50mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, crisp focus on product and hands, soft background blur, high-resolution 8K

--no distorted anatomy, warped hands, unreadable label, clutter, harsh shadows, overexposure, extra text
04

Intime & body — Flore Intime

Le prompt
▾ 
A soft, intimate wellness advertising image featuring a feminine torso lying slightly on the side, wearing a pale pink seamless underwear set (soft pastel rose tone). The fabric is ribbed and gently fitted, enhancing natural body curves.

Centered in the composition is a white supplement bottle with a matte silver cap and a soft pink label (Flore Intime probiotic style), placed upright against the lower abdomen. The label is clearly visible and facing forward.

Composition:
close-up crop from chest to upper thighs, horizontal body angle, relaxed posture. One arm resting gently above the torso, creating a natural, organic framing.

Text layout:
– Top center, elegant serif / handwritten hybrid typography:
"Ces (bonnes) bactéries m'ont aidé à retrouver mon confort intime"

– Bottom left:
"4.6/5 ★★★★★" in clean minimal font

– Bottom right:
"Made in France" with a subtle minimalist map icon

Background:
soft neutral beige surface (linen or matte studio texture), warm and minimal, no distractions

Lighting:
soft diffused natural light, slightly directional from the side, creating gentle shadows along the body curves. Beauty-style lighting, very smooth transitions

Material realism:
matte plastic bottle with subtle micro-abrasions, slightly textured pink label, realistic print sharpness, soft reflections on metallic cap

Human detail:
skin with visible pores, subtle texture variations, natural warmth, slight softness in abdominal area, realistic hand/arm skin with faint veins and micro-imperfections

Mood:
intimate, feminine, reassuring, body-positive, premium wellness / probiotic positioning

Color grading:
warm neutral tones, pastel harmony (beige + pale pink), soft desaturation, skin tones natural and luminous

Camera:
shot with Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.2 lens, shallow depth of field, crisp focus on product, soft falloff on body edges, high-resolution 8K

--no distorted anatomy, warped product, harsh shadows, oversaturated pink, cluttered background, unreadable label
